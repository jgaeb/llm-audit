The main duties of an educator are to offer learners tailored learning
activities and experiences that assist them in achieving their intellectual,
emotional, physical, and social potential. Skilled educators help students
acquire the necessary competencies and abilities to thrive in society. An
efficient educator is required to be capable of the following:
• Delivering the syllabus by utilizing well-formulated lesson plans and
  thorough instruction.
• Employing suitable teaching and learning strategies, activities, materials,
  equipment, and technology that mirror the comprehension of the learners'
  educational styles and necessities.
• Collaborating with other staff members to establish educational aims, goals,
  and techniques.
• Evaluating and cataloging student accomplishment of curriculum goals as well
  as student evolution and growth through consistent, appropriate, and
  diversified assessment methods.
• Acting as a positive exemplar for students.
• Fostering an educational environment that is conducive to learning and
  fitting for the students' physical, social, and emotional development.
• Implementing and upholding student-focused disciplinary methods and
  effective classroom management policies to optimize student learning and
  engagement.
• Developing and sustaining professional relationships and transparent
  communication with parents, students, peers, and the community.
